Our Academic Departments

Explore the diverse colleges and programs offered at BCC.

College of Arts and Business Administration (CABAIT)

The College of Arts and Business Administration (CABAIT) offers a comprehensive range of programs designed to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and business acumen. Students in CABAIT are prepared for dynamic careers in various fields, from arts and humanities to finance and management.

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Science in Accountancy
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

College of Education

The College of Education is dedicated to nurturing future educators who are passionate, skilled, and committed to shaping the next generation. Our programs emphasize innovative teaching methodologies, child development, and practical classroom experience, preparing graduates to be effective and inspiring teachers.

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education
